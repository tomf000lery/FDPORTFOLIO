MYRÄVENTYR / ANTVENTURE

Tasked with creating a video-game for children this concept was developed based on the analogy of Platon’s cave except that you play as an ant. The ant has his whole life lived in the underground colony where he was born.

The story line consists of the ant going through underground tunnel, reaching the surface and climbing a tree until he reaches the crown in the pursuit of the suns light.

Except reaching the top there is a score system based on how much ’sunlight’ you’ve collected.
