MYRÄVENTYR / ANTVENTURE

Tasked with creating a video-game for children this concept was developed based on the analogy of Plato’s cave except that you play as an ant.

The story follows an ant leaving its underground colony, reaching the surface and climbing a tree in pursuit of the sun.

Levels are built around puzzles, obstacles and a sunlight-based score system.
