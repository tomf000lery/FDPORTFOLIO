MYRÄVENTYR / ANTVENTURE

Tasked with creating a video-game for children this concept was developed based on the analogy of Platon’s cave except that you play as an ant. The ant has his whole life lived in the underground colony where he was born. Hearing legend and tales about something called the surface and ’The Sun’ he started to dream about getting out of the colony and out on an adventure.

The story line consists of the ant going through underground tunnel, reaching the surface and climbing a tree until he reaches the crown in the pursuit of the suns light. The levels are made up of small puzzles and obstacles that you have to pass to get to the next level.

Except reaching the top there is a score system based on how much ’sunlight’ you’ve collected that you’ve found scattered throughout the levels.
