Who's That?

I think it’s through storytelling that you best can communicate anything – whether it’s ideas, an emotion or a brand. I use it as my tool to craft lasting impressions and to spark conversation.

My name is Fahed Dehchar – I'm a born-and-raised renaissance man from Stockholm. But jokes aside and to be more specific: I am an illustrator, a graphic designer, animator, filmmaker and writer. I work both digitally and analog and my dream is to one day run my own publishing studio.

Feel free to hit me up if you want to collaborate, need help with a project or want to bounce ideas!
