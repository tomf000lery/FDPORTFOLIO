LUJAIN - ENEMI

In collaboration with Lujain for the release of her EP 'Vanguard' I produced this 1,5 minute long fight-scene vizualiser for the song ENEMI.

"The Boot-Licker Agenda"

Made during a course where the assigment was to be able to communicate a thought through any chosen artform about a subject. Perhaps controversial but I chose to formulate my thoughts on the (ethical) question about if billionaires should exist or not. This is the end result. .

Det Osagda - Kristin Rosendahl

In collaboration between Beckmans School of Design x The Royal College of Music in Stockholm I was teamed up with Kristin Rosendahl to create a minute long visualizer for one of her pieces.

VI, NI OCH DEM

For an assignment in school we were tasked with curating 100 pictures to bring up a subject. This work started with identity but ended up in social criticism. I chose to include it in my portfolio to showcase my ability in writing and storytelling.
